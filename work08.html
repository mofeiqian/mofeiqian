RI LI GUAN

WEB DESIGN

RI LI GUAN was a one of the first wave of online Chinese e-commerce store to hit the market back in 2011. The web store focused on supplying the modern Chinese young girls with affordable Japanese cosmetic products. In order to target the customers, the overall look and feel of the site was designed to be playful, vibrant and full of the taste of Japanese girl-magazine flavor. I rarely got the chance to design for such a style, and it was a fun and interesting progress.
