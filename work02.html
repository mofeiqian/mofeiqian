D3A

APP DESIGN

D3A is a cloud-based application that has the technical support to enables users to directly view and modify different type of files online individually or collectively. The application was first introduced as a partner app for Cisco Jabber in Windows platform, then soon expanded to stand alone app in both Windows, Mac and iOS platforms. I was the head designer to lead the overall design efforts through the entire production cycle.
