Royal Talens

This project for our design team was to assist the Netherlands-based paint brand Royal Talens to launch its country wide branding campaign of introducing products into the Chinese market in 2008. I was one of the graphic designers that worked on this mega scope for over 6 months. My works include design for packages, brochures, product photography and right down to the exhibition booth.
