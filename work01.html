APP DESIGN

GLANCE is an indoor location tracking application that shows registered mobile devices on maps. The application was initially contracted to be an one-off case for Cisco Live 2015 San Diego - Meet The Expert section to showcase Cisco’s CMX technology. The event was successful and GLANCE received many positive feedbacks which prompt the department to make it a stand alone product. I was the head designer for this project, and drove the product development to a full functional prototype from the initial ideation.
