A designer who loves to create and build ideas.

I believe that design is to bridge expression and structure, is about making ideas happen, finding solutions to a problem, and creating a experience to a moment. There is nothing more pleasant than witnessing my works delight its users and make the world functions a little bit better. I enjoy to draw, to read, to travel, to take pictures and everything else that make lives vivid and colourful. Keep on moving, keep on learning and keep on exploring something new.
